OUR MISSION

  • Initiative.
  • Empathy.
  • Open-mindedness.
As learners and storytellers living in place as teeming as Shanghai, we illustrate our home in all its rich nuance and history, and not simply by the modern velocity and glitz it is so famous for. To do this, we inquire deep into the overlooked stories of the city, giving these voices an amplifier and a platform to be expressed.

PROJECT I

Songs of the Alleyway《弄堂之歌》

A heart-warming, eclectic compilation of stories put together by our student interviewers, writers, artists, and editors. These 16 accounts each come from elders living across Shanghai’s many longtangs, or alleyway communities — including servicemen, solitaries, sages — with values to share, opinions to voice, and stories to tell.
